Faculty

Cong Shan

shan.cong@hrbeu.edu.cn

13045030309

Cong Shan, doctor of engineering, is mainly interested in developing and using AI algorithms to solve public health and medical problems. Focusing on Alzheimer's disease (AD) and other complex degenerative cognitive diseases, the deep pathogenic mechanism of major brain diseases and its specific impact on brain structure have been explored through medical image analysis, image gene association analysis, brain connectivity and brain network analysis.
