Positions

We are looking for highly motivated students and scholars to work with our group on advanced biomedical informatics technology development and application. We especially seek for candidates with a background in informatics, computing, engineering, or a related quantitative field, a record of academic productivities, proficiency in programming (e.g., using RMatlabPython/C), and a demonstrated interest in biomedical informatics.

We are affiliated with the following PhD programs at Penn.

  • AMCS: Graduate Group in Applied Math and Computational Science

  • BioEng: Graduate Group in Bioengineering

  • GCB: Graduate Group in Genomics and Computational Biology

  • GGEB: Graduate Group in Epidemiology and Biostatistics

We currently have positions available for Postdoctoral Fellows, Graduate Students (PhD, Masters), and Undergraduates.

  • Positions for Postdocs/Students who are interested in working in our research fields.

  • Postdoc Position: Imaging Genomics

  • Undergraduate Position (NSF REU): Mining Brain Imaging Genetics Data

Interested parties can contact Dr. Shen for more details.
