Positions

We are looking for highly motivated students and scholars to work with our group on advanced biomedical informatics technology development and application. We especially seek for candidates with a background in informatics, statistics, computer science, or a related quantitative field, a record of academic productivities, proficiency in programming (e.g., using R/Matlab/Python/C++/Java/Javascript), and a demonstrated interest in biomedical informatics.

We are affiliated with the following PhD programs at Harbin Engineering University.

  • CE: Graduate Group in Control Systems Science and Engineering

  • IE: Graduate Group in Information Engineering

  • BME: Graduate Group in Biomedical Engineering

We currently have positions available for Postdoctoral Fellows, Graduate Students (PhD, Masters), and Undergraduates.

  • Positions for Postdocs/Students who are interested in working in our research fields.

  • PhD Position: Imaging Genomics

  • Undergraduate Position: Computer Vision, Mining Brain Imaging Genetics Data

Interested parties can contact Dr. Cong for more details.
